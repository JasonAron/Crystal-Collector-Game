Crystal Collector

Click On The Gems To Play

1: You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2: There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amout of points to your total score.

3: You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you klose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4: The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

5: Each time when the game starts, the game will change the valie of each crystal.
